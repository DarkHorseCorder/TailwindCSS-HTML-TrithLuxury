PHONE CONSULTATION

Interested in buying a property in Turkey?
Finding a nice apartment for sale in Turkey can be difficult especially if you live outside the country. For this reason, we provide you with a consultation service to guide through the real estate sector in Turkey and to help you make the right decisions by assigning you professional real estate consultants who will offer you the most appropriate advice and answer all your questions through direct messages, phone calls and emails.
How does the consultation work?
Once you reach out to us, our professional team of investment consultants will be ready to assist you by providing you with the most efficient information and helping you choose and plan your investment precisely in accordance with your needs, preferences and budget. The consultation process is made in your native language for better and more effective communication. Once we completely understand your expectations, we start recommending potential projects and advantageous options that match your preferences and assist you in decision.

LEGAL CONSULTING SERVICES

What legal services do we offer?
Once you set your mind on a project and chose a suitable investment plan according to your needs and preferences, our legal department offers you the possibility to handle the legal process. We oversee the various procedures related to your investment and we make sure that everything is running smoothly.

Our legal services include payment procedures and financial matters, providing legal advice, preparing contracts, initiating and following-up on title deed process, application for the investment certificate, etc.

To sum-up, we basically act as an intermediate between the customer and the real estate developer. Our legal department’s ultimate goal is to finish the purchases operation and the different legal procedures relating to it while guaranteeing your rights and making sure you are satisfied.
